ReBound Together is part of the Bound Together brand.
Here at ReBound Together, we are dedicated to helping you complete your book collection through our great selection of second hand books
As part of the Bound Together family, when using this app, you also have the availability to use your same log in details to purchase new books.

How It Works

If you enjoy reading books

Join our app and when you have finished with a book, list it for sale and purchase another one.

And keep that chain going until you cannot find anything else to read. (Yeah right!)

If you enjoy collecting books

Have a look through our great database and see if there are any books that spark your interest.

Need to hide your purchases? when checking out on the app, ask if the books can be sent in an incospicuos way. (As if that is an option)

Prefer New Books?

Check out our sister site Bound Together and check out their collection.
